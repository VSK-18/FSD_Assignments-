Welcome to Swaad

Where Tradition Meets Elegance

Established in 2005 in the heart of Chinchwad, Maharashtra, Swaad brings you the finest authentic recipes passed down through generations.

Our chefs use only the freshest locally sourced ingredients and traditional spices imported directly from India, creating dishes that are a feast for both the eyes and the palate.
